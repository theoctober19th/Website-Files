== Description ==

Anna Lite is an elegant and clean WordPress theme, perfect to share your stories, sell your products and manage your fashion or food blog. Based on Bootstrap framework, Anna Lite is optimized to work with Contact Form 7 and WooCommerce plugins.

PDF documentation available on the link below:
https://demo.themeinprogress.com/anna/anna-lite-documentation.pdf
